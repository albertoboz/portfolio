What is your design process?

  1. Learn all I can about the target audience.
  2. Find out the format and budgetary constraints.
  3. Brainstorm design solutions and sketch out several quick ideas.
  4. Develop the roughs. Get feedback from the team or client on which is most successful for their needs.
  5. Use the last round of feedback to polish up the project into the finished product.
